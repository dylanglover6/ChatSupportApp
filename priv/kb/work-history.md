## Axure Software — Product Support Lead

**Austin, TX · Feb 2022 – Mar 2026**

Owned Tier II/III technical support for enterprise SaaS customers — the escalations that got
past the first line. That meant living in APIs, webhooks, integrations, account
configuration, and installation behavior, and diagnosing issues through log review, API
response inspection, browser/server behavior analysis, and screen-share troubleshooting.

A lot of the real value was in translation: turning ambiguous customer reports into
actionable bug reports and escalation packets — observed vs. expected behavior, customer
impact, environment details, reproduction steps, logs — and partnering with Engineering and
QA to reproduce edge cases, validate fixes, and communicate resolution timelines. Held 95%+
CSAT across 50+ weekly tickets while authoring the customer-facing KB articles and internal
runbooks that reduced repeat contacts on known issues. Also worked with Sales and Customer
Success on technical pre-sales questions, integration scoping, and escalation triage for
enterprise accounts.

## MadCap Software — Technical Support Lead

**Austin, TX · Feb 2019 – Feb 2022**

Led technical support for enterprise customers across complex installations, publishing
workflows, system configurations, and web-based product behavior — including HTML/CSS output
configuration and REST API integrations. Built the structured troubleshooting guides,
escalation workflows, and repeatable support procedures that improved resolution
consistency and cut onboarding ramp time for new support hires.

Mentored new support team members and contributed to training materials, building team-wide
depth on common failure patterns and customer environments. Spent a lot of time at the
seam between technical and non-technical audiences — explaining integration behavior and
environment-specific troubleshooting clearly to whoever was on the other end — and worked
cross-functionally to surface recurring product issues and route well-scoped feedback to the
right internal teams.
