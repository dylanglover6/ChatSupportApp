# Achievements

  runbooks, fixing the *class* of problem, not just the instance, so the same ticket
  stopped coming back.
- **Shipped this platform** (a working Phoenix/LiveView application with real-time chat,
  AI-assisted triage, and a full support-desk workflow) while actively learning the

- **Built AI support tooling hands-on**: RAG-style KB search, automatic ticket triage, and
  LLM-generated reply suggestions against a local model, bringing real support-domain
  knowledge to an AI product, not the other way around.
- **Microsoft Azure Fundamentals (AZ-900)**: in progress, alongside actually deploying this
  site to Azure.
